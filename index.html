Capabilities

What we deliver.

We staff and support federal construction and safety programs with credentialed personnel, audit-grade documentation, and the operational discipline expected by NAVFAC, USACE, and Department of Defense prime contractors.

01 / Staffing

SSHO Staffing

Site Safety & Health Officers credentialed to EM 385-1-1, with verified Competent Person certifications across fall protection, excavation, scaffolding, and confined space.

02 / Compliance

EM 385-1-1 Compliance

Pre-construction APP/AHA development, daily safety reporting, near-miss tracking, and accident prevention plan oversight aligned with USACE and NAVFAC requirements.

03 / Subcontracting

Federal Subcontracting

NAVFAC Pacific subcontractor partnerships supporting safety, training, and project support roles — operating under FAR-compliant agreements and SDVOSB self-performance rules.

04 / Training

Safety Training Curriculum

Building a sanctioned safety training pipeline through accreditation partnerships — from OSHA fundamentals to advanced federal construction safety curricula.

05 / Sourcing

DLA & Federal Supply

Strategic positioning on DLA Aviation and federal supply solicitations as an SDVOSB set-aside vendor, with established supplier relationships and FAR-aligned bid response.

06 / Operations

GovCon Infrastructure

FAR 4.703-compliant document retention, DFARS flow-down management, and contract administration discipline — built for audit, scaled for growth.

About

Veteran-led.
Pacific-grounded.

TR Global LLC was founded to bring credentialed safety professionals and federal contracting discipline to the Pacific construction theater — under the unconditional ownership and control of a service-disabled veteran.

From our headquarters in Kapolei, Hawai‘i, we operate at the intersection of federal construction safety and small-business excellence. Every engagement we accept is structured around two non-negotiables: SDVOSB program integrity and audit-ready documentation.

We work alongside prime contractors who serve NAVFAC Pacific, USACE, and Department of Defense customers — placing Site Safety & Health Officers on active job sites, supporting safety program development, and absorbing the operational complexity that primes need pushed off their plate.

Our growth roadmap extends beyond staffing: a sanctioned safety training curriculum, prime contractor licensing milestones, and a long-horizon vision to build the operational backbone the federal small-business community is missing.
